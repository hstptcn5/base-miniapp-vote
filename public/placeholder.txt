# Public Assets

Place your app assets here:
- icon.png - App icon (recommended: 512x512px)
- screenshot.png - Screenshot for app store (recommended: 1284x2778px for portrait)
- splash.png - Splash screen
- hero.png - Hero image for embeds
- og-image.png - Open Graph image for social sharing
- logo.png - Logo for wallet modal

You can use the Mini App Assets Generator: https://www.miniappassets.com/
